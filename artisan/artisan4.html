Marcel Duchamp

Biography

Marcel Duchamp was a highly influential creator.
His work has shaped much of the discourse surrounding modern art.

He was born in Blainville-Crevon, France during 1887.
Eventually, he chose to become an American citizen. Later, he passed away within his home country in 1968.

Duchamp is largely remembered for his impactful contributions to Cubism and conceptual art movements during the early to mid 20th century. He pioneered notions of 'readymade' art, intended to communicate ideas beyond purely visual aesthetics.
