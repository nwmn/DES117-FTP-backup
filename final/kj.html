Cut + Paste Montage

Software Used: Adobe Illustrator

This work encompasses a lighthearted and humorous investigation of visual motifs and underlying production strategies found in postmodernist memes. The piece is intended to demonstrate process over end product. Public speculation surrounding the currently unknown, deliberately withheld identity of the constituents making up Terror JR, a new pop music group is the central subject matter portrayed. Unsubstantiated rumors about Terror JR that now rampantly circulate around various internet spheres reminded me of a similar framework depicted in Hannah Montana, a fabled television drama glorifying teenage stardom and celebrity culture. In line with public discourse on this matter, raster production centered around transplanting a recent image of the primary suspect's face onto a promotional advert of Miley Cyrus dressed as Hannah Montana, circa 2007.
